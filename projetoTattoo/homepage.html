What we do

Since 1989, our studio has been a cornerstone of creativity and self-expression. We specialize in custom tattoos and professional piercings, combining traditional techniques with modern artistry to bring your visions to life. For over three decades, we have been committed to excellence in the art of tattooing and body piercing. Our long-standing reputation is built on trust, creativity, and a passion for helping our clients express themselves. Join us at our studio and become part of our enduring legacy of artistic innovation.

Our team of experienced tattoo artists is dedicated to crafting unique, personalized designs that reflect your individuality. Whether you're looking for intricate black and grey work, vibrant color pieces, or detailed line art.

Our professional piercers are skilled in a variety of piercing styles, from classic ear piercings to more complex body modifications. We provide personalized care and aftercare advice to help you achieve the perfect look.
